Tune Me In

Welcome to Tune Me In! Want to look into a new band you just heard on the radio, or just check up on some information on one of your favorites? Or maybe you want to quickly grab a link to a music video you love to show a friend. Using the YouTube and last.fm API's, you can search for videos and biographical information for almost any musician you can think of. Fill out the forms below, click search, and watch the cards below populate. Clicking each video thumbnail will open the video in a new tab, and you can save the link to any video by clicking the copy button.
